National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) - National Cardiovascular Disease Surveillance System
	
	https://healthdata.gov/search/field_tags/cardiovascular-disease-281

This is one of the datasets provided by the National Cardiovascular Disease Surveillance System. 
The system is designed to integrate multiple indicators from many data sources to provide a comprehensive picture 
of the public health burden of CVDs and associated risk factors in the United States. The data can be plotted as 
trends and stratified by age group, sex, and race/ethnicity.
